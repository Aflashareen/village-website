Politician & NRI Contributor

Visitor Information

  • Getting Here: Vengara is accessible by road from Malappuram (20 km) and Kozhikode (35 km). The nearest railway station is Tirur (25 km), and the nearest airport is Calicut International Airport (30 km).
  • Accommodation: Options include local homestays, guesthouses, and hotels in nearby Malappuram and Kozhikode.
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March for pleasant weather, ideal for exploring outdoor attractions.
  • Guided Tours: Contact our visitor center for guided village tours and cultural experiences.
